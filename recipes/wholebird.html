Roast Chicken

Description

Roast chicken is a classic dish that is both comforting and satisfying. It begins with a whole chicken, which is seasoned with herbs, salt, and pepper, and then roasted in the oven until the skin is crispy and golden brown and the meat is juicy and tender.

Ingredients

  • Whole Bird
  • Butter
  • Seasoning

Steps

  1. Wash the chicken under cold water
  2. Butter the cavity
  3. Generously season the outside of the chicken, including the cavity
  4. Tie the legs together around the ankles with kitchen thread
  5. Tuck in the wings
  6. Cook at 190℃ for 45 minutes
  7. Allow to cool for 5 minutes
